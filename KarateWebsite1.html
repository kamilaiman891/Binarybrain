    OUR TRAININGS

    Karate training offers numerous benefits for both physical and mental health. Here in SeishinKan Dojo, we teach our students the way of Shito-Ryu Karate-do to enhance the ability to defend oneself in dangerous situations. Moreover, we also provide continual encouragement and feedback, which builds studentsâ€™ self-confidence levels as well as proper technique. A typical karate training class will usually begin with a warm-up session to prepare the body for the physical activity that follows. This may include stretching exercises, jogging, or jumping jacks. After the warm-up, the instructor will usually demonstrate and explain the techniques that will be covered in the class. This may include basic strikes, kicks, blocks, and stances. Students will then practice these techniques with a partner or on their own, under the guidance of the instructor. The instructor will provide feedback and corrections to help students improve their form and technique. As the class progresses, the instructor may introduce more advanced techniques or combinations, and students will continue to practice and refine their skills. Towards the end of the class, there may be a cool-down period where students stretch and relax their muscles. The instructor may also lead a brief meditation or breathing exercise to help students focus and calm their minds.
